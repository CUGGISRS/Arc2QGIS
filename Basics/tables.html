How To Open The Attributes Table

You have three easy ways to open the Attributes Table:

  1. Right click the layer you want to open, and select Open Attribute Table.


  2. In the toolbar under the menus click on the table icon and the attribute table of the current working layer will open.



  3. Select a working table and press F6, thats it.
